I am currently a postdoctoral research at Scripps Institution of Oceanography.

I use numerical and statistical modeling to study freshwater processes in the upper ocean. My current work is aimed at using machine learning to understand how the dynamical properties of sea ice in the Arctic are changing as the ice melts. I employ explainable AI (XAI) techniques to understand changes in the drivers of variability of ice motion, with particular focus given to the changing relationship between wind and ice speed.
